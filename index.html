Teaching Thoery

Weekly studio and specialized workshop

As an experienced art tutor with a passion for fostering creativity, I have spent over 12 years working with students of all ages, helping them discover and develop their artistic potential. My background includes a Master’s degree in Landscape Architecture, along with specialized training in digital art, such as painting, drawing, and mixed media. I have worked in a variety of educational settings, from private studios to afterschool program, and traditional classrooms.

My teaching philosophy is centered on encouraging individuality and self-expression while providing students with a strong foundation in the fundamentals of art. During my classes, I guide students through the creative process, focusing on both art skills and creative expression. Whether working with children, teens, or adults, I believe in creating a supportive environment where each student feels inspired to take risks and explore their unique artistic voice.

Class Schedule

Color-pencil Drawing

  • Introduction to lines (straight, curved, zigzag, dotted, etc.)
  • Create visual textures using patterns and repetition.
  • Project: Drawing a line art cityscape or landscape.

Acrylic Techniques

  • Basic acrylic techniques: wet-on-wet, dry brush, and blending.
  • Explore abstract art and expressive techniques.
  • Project: Paint a simple landscape or abstract design using acrylic techniques.

Drawing with Perspective

  • Introduction to one-point perspective for creating depth.
  • Project: Draw simple street scenes or buildings using perspective.
  • Project: Create a color wheel using paint or colored pencils.
